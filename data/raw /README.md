# Raw Data Description

This directory contains metadata for the raw sequencing dataset used in the pharmacogenomics analysis of praziquantel metabolism in Zimbabwean populations.

---

## Study Overview

- **Study Title:** Pharmacogenetics of CYP450 involved in praziquantel metabolism in Zimbabweans  
- **BioProject:** PRJNA897802  
- **BioProject Study ID:** SRP406043  
- **Experiment ID:** SRX18143285  
- **Sample ID:** SAMN31580365 / SRS15644640  
- **Organism:** Homo sapiens  
- **Institution:** University of Edinburgh  
- **Study Type:** Whole Exome Sequencing (WXS)  
- **Focus:** CYP450 genomic regions relevant to drug metabolism

---

## Sequencing Platform

- **Instrument:** Illumina HiSeq 4000  
- **Strategy:** Whole Exome Sequencing (WXS)  
- **Library Preparation:** PCR-based selection  
- **Layout:** Paired-end sequencing

## Dataset Summary

- Total reads: ~24.2 million paired-end spots  
- Total sequencing output: 7.3 gigabases  
- Download size: 4.2 GB  
- Primary focus genes: CYP450 family (pharmacogenetic relevance to praziquantel metabolism)

---

## Notes for Reproducibility

- This dataset is part of a pharmacogenetic analysis pipeline involving:
  - Quality control
  - Read trimming
  - Alignment
  - Variant calling (VCF generation)
  - Clinical annotation

- Downstream analysis focuses on identifying genetic variants that may influence drug metabolism variability in praziquantel response.
